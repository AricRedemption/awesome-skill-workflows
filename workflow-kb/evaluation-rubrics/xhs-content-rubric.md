# XHS Content Rubric

## Scope

Use this rubric for Xiaohongshu AI tools, workflow, and productivity content. It evaluates the content package before draft or publish handoff.

## Score Dimensions

- Topic attraction: Does the topic have a concrete reader promise?
- Pain point clarity: Does the first screen name a recognizable pain?
- Title and cover clickability: Are title and cover specific, oral, and non-duplicative?
- Xiaohongshu voice fit: Does the copy feel useful and conversational rather than corporate?
- Body structure: Is the post scannable, with sections that progress logically?
- Save value: Does the reader get a reusable template, checklist, or repeatable method?
- Hashtag fit: Are tags relevant and not spammy?
- Interaction hook: Is the comment prompt natural and scenario-based?
- Compliance risk: Does the post avoid exaggerated claims, privacy issues, and unsupported data?

## Passing Rules

- Numeric content score can pass while publish readiness fails.
- Human review is required for publish readiness.
- Compliance score must pass before any platform handoff.
- A high save-value score is not a replacement for account-state verification.

## Recommended Thresholds

- Scenario content score: `>= 90`
- Compliance score: `>= 90`
- Platform fit score: `>= 85`
- Publish readiness: `human_review_passed == true` and `account_state == ready_for_handoff`

## Review Questions

- Would the target reader know what to do today?
- Is the promise bounded and not exaggerated?
- Does the post include a copyable or collectible unit?
- Are title and cover aligned without repeating the same sentence?
- Can the output be converted into a draft without missing fields?
