# Risk Boundary

- 不自动发布真实内容。
- 发布或保存草稿前必须 human review。
- 发布或保存草稿前必须确认账号登录和授权状态。
- 不自动点赞、评论、关注。
- 不做大规模爬取。
- 不采集隐私数据。
- 合规风险不达标必须进入 revision。
- 发布失败时必须 fallback 到 draft-only。

## Boundary Summary

This scenario is limited to human-reviewed content preparation, account-state checking, draft/package creation, verification, and knowledge capture. Any action that would change a real Xiaohongshu account state without review is out of scope.
