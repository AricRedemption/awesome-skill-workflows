# Risk Boundary

- Do not automatically publish real content.
- Human review is required before saving a draft or publishing.
- Account login and authorization must be confirmed before draft or publish handoff.
- Do not automate likes, comments, follows, account farming, or engagement manipulation.
- Do not perform large-scale scraping.
- Do not collect private data.
- If compliance risk fails, the workflow must enter revision.
- If publish handoff fails, use the approved draft-only fallback.

## Boundary Summary

This scenario is limited to human-reviewed content preparation, account-state checking, draft or package creation, verification, and knowledge capture. Any action that changes a real Xiaohongshu account state without review is out of scope.
